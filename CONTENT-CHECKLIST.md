# Production Content Checklist

Replace or confirm the following before the website is published:

## Identity

- Final legal school name
- Final crest and wordmark
- Official motto wording
- Brand usage approval

## Contact information

- Campus address
- Main telephone number
- Admissions telephone number
- Official email addresses
- Office hours
- Social media links
- Verified map coordinates

## School information

- Founding year
- Student and staff counts
- Mission, vision and values
- Principal or head of school name and approved message
- Leadership and teacher profiles
- Curriculum and subject list
- Departments
- Facilities
- Clubs and activities

## Admissions

- Entry levels
- Age requirements
- Application documents
- Assessment process
- Current fees
- Payment details
- Scholarships or financial support
- Enrolment dates and availability

## Policies

- Admissions policy
- Attendance policy
- Discipline and behaviour policy
- Child protection and safeguarding policy
- Privacy policy
- Terms of use
- Photo and media consent policy
- Complaints and support process

## Media

- Approved campus photographs
- Classroom photographs
- Staff headshots
- Student activity photographs with consent
- Achievement and event images
- Image alt text

## Portal and technology

- Backend platform
- Secure authentication
- User provisioning process
- Parent-child account linking
- Grade and attendance workflow
- Notification delivery method
- Data backup and recovery
- Security testing
- Mobile and browser testing
- Accessibility review
